Education Support: Empowering the Next Generation

Investing in Education Across Uganda

We believe education is key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Salimu Saley Muslim Charity supports students and schools across Uganda by providing scholarships, learning materials, and infrastructure improvements.

Our initiatives include:

  • Scholarships for talented but needy students
  • Supplying books, stationery, and technology
  • Building and renovating classrooms
  • Training teachers and organizing educational workshops
