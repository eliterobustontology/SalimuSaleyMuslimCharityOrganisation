Food Aid: Nourishing Lives Across Uganda

Our Impact Through Food Aid

At Salimu Saley Muslim Charity, we provide vital food assistance to impoverished families across Uganda. Hunger and food insecurity remain major challenges affecting millions. Through coordinated food distributions, we ensure that families receive nutritious staples and fresh produce to sustain their health and dignity.

Our work includes:

  • Monthly food parcel distributions
  • Emergency relief for drought and conflict-affected areas
  • Partnering with local organizations for sustainable food programs
  • Education on nutrition and food preservation
